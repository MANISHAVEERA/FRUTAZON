Frutazon is all about cold-pressed juices and smoothies, both fruits and vegetables flavoured with spices that entice the taste buds. With a wide variety to choose from, we ensure that they are garden fresh, untouched, unadulterated and in the most elite form, suitable to be consumed by all.
